## Surface Preparation

Proper preparation is essential for commercial painting projects. Without it, paint durability and appearance may be affected.

Common preparation steps include:
- Cleaning surfaces thoroughly
- Removing old or damaged paint
- Repairing cracks and imperfections
- Sanding rough areas
- Applying primer coatings

As a result, properly prepared surfaces ensure long-lasting and professional finishes.
